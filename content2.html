HEALTHY LAWN ANALYSIS®


The TruGreen Healthy Lawn Analysis® is the first step of your Healthy Lawn Plan.

A TruGreen specialist will visit your house and conduct a detailed lawn analysis. He will evaluate variables such as climate, grass and soil types. Extra consideration will be given to problem areas, diseases, soil compaction, pet damage, and shade and sun exposure.

The TruGreen Healthy Lawn Analysis gives the specialist a valuable understanding of your lawn’s strengths and challenges, and serves as the basis of a customized plan that will help your lawn reach its maximum potential.

WEED CONTROL

 

Weeds are simply defined as unwanted plants.

Typically, weeds that are growing well in your lawn are native plants that have room to grow in the environmental or cultural conditions that favor their development. TruGreen Healthy Lawn Analysis will identify what type of weeds are growing in your backyard – weeds such as dandelions and crabgrass. No single herbicide, weeding technique or lawn care tactic works against all weeds. Only TruGreen knows how to customize a treatment plan based on the exact types of weeds in your lawn. That’s why weed control is built into your customized year-round TruGreen Healthy Lawn Plan.

The results you receive from the TruGreen Healthy Lawn Analysis will recommend the optimal time to apply pre-emergent weed control to help keep weeds from showing up in the first place. Additionally, TruGreen follows up with applications throughout the year to eliminate weeds that do pop up and keep them from spreading.

—The sound lawn care practices TruGreen provides will promote a healthy, vigorous turf which is able to prevent and compete with weed invasions.
